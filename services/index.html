Questions people usually ask

Do you work with businesses outside Switzerland?

Yes. Most work is done remotely. We also check whether the tools make sense for your location and way of working.

How long does a typical project take?

Small fixes can take days. Larger setups usually take a few weeks. I will tell you early what looks realistic.

Do I need a budget for tools or ads?

Not always. We start with what you already use and only add tools or ad spend when it clearly helps.

What if I only need one specific thing fixed?

That is fine. We can fix the one thing blocking you and keep the rest out of scope.
