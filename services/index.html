Services

Your service is solid. Your setup still leaks clients.

I fix the parts that make good businesses look unfinished: weak websites, messy enquiries, broken follow-up, and manual systems that waste time.

Stage 01

Launch properly

The first layer of setup that makes the business look serious and easy to contact.

This stage is about getting the essentials right: a proper website, business email, contact form, booking when needed, Google Business Profile, and indexing so people can actually find you.

  • website setup in Squarespace, Wix, or code depending on fit
  • service page structure and clearer messaging
  • forms, booking flow, and contact setup
  • domain, business email, sitemap, and indexing basics
  • Google Business Profile and practical visibility groundwork

Stage 02

Track enquiries properly

The first simple systems that stop enquiries from living in inboxes, DMs, and memory.

Once the website is live, the next question is simple: who enquired, what happened next, and what is turning into paid work?

  • starter CRM or database structure
  • pipeline stages and clear next steps after an enquiry
  • simple reporting habits and visibility into lead flow
  • Stripe, invoicing, and payment setup when relevant
  • ad-readiness for Google Ads or Meta once the basics are stable

Stage 03

Tighten delivery and reporting

Later-stage cleanup for businesses where delivery, handovers, and reporting are starting to get messy.

This is where HubSpot and stronger workflow design start to make sense. Not on day one. Later, when volume and complexity actually justify it.

  • HubSpot starter setup and cleaner pipeline structure
  • better handovers, ownership, and follow-up routines
  • clearer reporting once there is enough activity to measure
  • workflow cleanup when the business starts feeling scattered

Process

Three steps. Less guesswork. Better follow-through.

We fix the weak points first, put the right setup in place, and keep improving once the business has real data to work with.

01

Audit & clarity

We review the website, enquiry path, tools, and follow-up. You leave with a clear list of what is broken, what matters now, and what can wait.

02

System setup

I build the pieces that remove friction: website fixes, forms, booking, CRM basics, payments, and the handoffs behind them.

03

Optimization & reporting

Once things are live, we track what is coming in, tighten the weak spots, and use simple reporting to make the next decision obvious.

Working style

Build what you need now. Add heavier systems later.

I do not push people into heavyweight software or a long consulting process before the business is ready. The work should match the stage you are actually in.

That means building the website and first workflow properly, then adding more structure only when real lead flow or client load justifies it.

Strong fit when

You need one person who can connect the website, enquiries, Google presence, booking, and payments without overcomplicating it.

Not the first move when

You mainly need an enterprise CRM rollout, a large development team, or a strategy document with no implementation.
