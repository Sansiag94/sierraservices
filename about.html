About

Santiago Sierra

I help service businesses bring more structure to the way they attract leads, manage opportunities, and run their day-to-day operations.

I am based in Zurich and hold a Master's degree in Business Administration from Hochschule Luzern. My work sits at the intersection of operations, digital systems, and practical business execution.

Over the years I have worked with service businesses that reached a stage where growth exposed weak processes, unclear handovers, scattered information, or underused systems. In many cases, the problem was not effort. It was the absence of a clear operating structure.

What I do best is translate that complexity into something manageable. That can mean clarifying website messaging, structuring a CRM pipeline, documenting workflows, improving analytics, or making reporting more useful for decision-making.

I work best with founders and small teams who want practical improvements, clear prioritization, and systems that support real daily use rather than impressive but unnecessary complexity.

How I Work

  • clear prioritization over bloated transformation projects
  • systems designed around daily execution, not theory
  • documentation that improves consistency and handover quality
  • straightforward communication in English and Spanish
