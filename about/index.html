About

Direct help for founders who want the basics done properly

Sierra Services is led by Santiago Sierra in Zurich. I help founders turn a skill into a business people can understand and contact: clear website, clear offer, clear next steps.

I hold a Master's degree in Business Administration from Hochschule Luzern. Useful foundation. But clients hire me for execution: clearer pages, cleaner flows, and business systems that make day-to-day work easier.

A lot of founders already know their craft. What they do not have yet is the structure around it. No proper site. No clean enquiry path. No business email. No simple way to track leads and next steps.

That is the gap I like fixing. The work can include the website, Google presence, forms, booking, Stripe or invoicing setup, and the first simple systems that stop the business from running on memory alone.

What people tend to value

Clear work, clear communication, and proper follow-through

I have not filled this site with polished testimonial quotes. What I can say honestly is that the same points keep coming up.

Clear paths

People like knowing what to do next instead of leaving with abstract advice.

Plain language

The work is explained in a way clients can follow without decoding jargon.

Responsibility

Tasks are handled seriously, with follow-through, not as disconnected gigs.

Help across moving parts

It helps when the website, forms, booking, payments, and systems all need to work together.

How I work

Direct, practical, and not overbuilt

  • you work directly with the person doing the work
  • recommendations matched to the stage your business is actually in
  • implementation alongside guidance, not strategy alone
  • systems that should still make sense six months later
