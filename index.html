FAQ

Common Questions Before We Speak

Do I need a new website to work with you?

Not necessarily. Many projects begin by improving structure, messaging, analytics, or conversion flow on an existing site before any rebuild is considered.

Can you work with the CRM and tools we already use?

Yes. I usually start with your current setup and improve the way it is configured, documented, and used before suggesting bigger tool changes.

Is this relevant for a small team?

Yes. This type of work is often most useful for founders and small teams that need more structure without becoming heavy or bureaucratic.

What happens in the first consultation?

We review the current bottlenecks, clarify priorities, and decide whether a focused engagement would make sense and what the first actions should be.
