Why Clients Get In Touch

Most enquiries start when the business has outgrown its current structure

The issue is rarely just one tool. More often, the website, CRM, and internal workflow have developed separately and no longer support the business in a clear way.

The goal of the work is to make those parts more coherent, more usable, and easier to manage.

  • Leads come in, but follow-up is inconsistent.
  • The CRM exists, but pipeline stages or ownership are unclear.
  • The website does not clearly support enquiries or credibility.
  • Important processes depend too much on memory and improvisation.
  • Reporting exists, but it is not helping with day-to-day decisions.

Representative Work

The kind of problems I am usually asked to untangle

These are typical engagement patterns, not polished case studies. The work usually starts when the business is functioning, but not in a way that feels stable or easy to manage.

Process

A practical way of working

I keep projects focused and grounded in what the business actually needs, rather than turning everything into a larger transformation than necessary.

Review

Assess the current setup

We start by understanding where the process is unclear, where the handovers are weak, and which tools are underperforming.

Prioritise

Decide what matters first

The first priority is usually the area that most directly affects enquiries, follow-up quality, or operational visibility.

Implement

Build a clearer operating structure

The result should be easier to use and easier to maintain, with documentation and structure that support the team after the project ends.

Best fit

Usually a strong fit if

  • the business already has traction, but the operating structure has not kept up
  • you want direct work on the actual system, not just strategic advice
  • the website, CRM, and delivery process are drifting apart and need to be reconnected
  • you need more clarity without turning the business into something bureaucratic

Less likely

Probably not the right first move if

  • you mainly need a large design or development team right away
  • the offer itself is still changing too much to build structure around it yet
  • you only want a high-level strategy document with no implementation work
  • the core issue is accounting or billing rather than consulting, in which case Sierra Invoices is the better fit
