Why Clients Get In Touch

Most enquiries start when the business has outgrown its current structure

The issue is rarely just one tool. More often, the website, CRM, and internal workflow have developed separately and no longer support the business in a clear way.

The goal of the work is to make those parts more coherent, more usable, and easier to manage.

  • Leads come in, but follow-up is inconsistent.
  • The CRM exists, but pipeline stages or ownership are unclear.
  • The website does not clearly support enquiries or credibility.
  • Important processes depend too much on memory and improvisation.
  • Reporting exists, but it is not helping with day-to-day decisions.
